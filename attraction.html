Attraction

As-Salam Mosque and Puchong Indah National School stand as key attractions in Puchong Perdana. As-Salam Mosque, also known as the Floating Mosque, showcases stunning architecture by the lake, offering a serene space for worship and community gatherings. On the other hand, Puchong Indah National School serves as a vital educational institution, providing primary education to students in the area. These landmarks contribute to the vibrancy and identity of Puchong Perdana, enriching the community and attracting visitors with their distinct offerings and significance.

As-Salam Mosque

The As-Salam Mosque, affectionately termed the Floating Mosque, stands as an iconic architectural wonder in Malaysia. Nestled gracefully by the tranquil shores of a man-made lake in Puchong Perdana, Selangor, it epitomizes the common tradition of situating mosques near water bodies to harness the cooling effects amidst Malaysia's warm climate. Officially unveiled in 2006 by Sultan Sharafuddin Idris Shah of Selangor, this majestic structure has garnered acclaim as one of Malaysia's most remarkable mosques. Comprehensively equipped with amenities such as a primary prayer hall, a dedicated space for female worshippers, administrative offices, a travelers' lodge, and versatile meeting facilities, the mosque caters to the diverse needs of its local community. Nestled adjacent to a verdant public park and nestled amidst educational institutions and residential enclaves, its three grand domes and soaring minaret have become quintessential symbols visible from afar. Inspired by a fusion of Arabian, Byzantine, and Ottoman architectural influences, the mosque's design features elegant pointed arches, intricate Islamic geometrical patterns, and a contemporary postmodern flair. Internally, the main prayer hall is bathed in natural light, complemented by thoughtfully positioned windows and strategically placed free-standing pillars fitted with mechanical fans for optimal ventilation. Adorned with exquisite tile work and a meticulously embellished mihrab wall, the hall exudes an atmosphere of tranquility conducive to devout reflection and prayer. Seamlessly blending traditional Islamic motifs with modern design sensibilities, the As-Salam Mosque stands as a testament to Malaysia's cultural richness and architectural innovation.

Puchong Indah National School

Puchong Indah National School is an educational institution situated in the Puchong area of Selangor, Malaysia. As a government-funded national school, it caters to the primary education needs of students in the surrounding community. The school provides a comprehensive curriculum aligned with national standards, aiming to foster academic excellence and holistic development among its students. With a focus on academic achievement, character building, and extracurricular engagement, Sekolah Kebangsaan Puchong Indah plays a vital role in shaping the future generation of Malaysian citizens.
