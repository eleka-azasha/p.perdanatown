Accessibility

In Puchong Perdana and neighboring areas, accessibility to destinations across the Klang Valley is greatly facilitated by the Damansara-Puchong Expressway (LDP) and the Shah Alam Expressway (KESAS), offering residents convenient connections throughout the region.


Puchong is connected to other areas of the Klang Valley, including as Damansara, Petaling Jaya, and Kuala Lumpur, by the LDP. The highway provides Puchong inhabitants with an easy and quick means to commute to work, school, shopping malls, and other locations in the Klang Valley. In general, Puchong's accessibility and connectivity to the larger Klang Valley region are greatly improved by the Damansara-Puchong Expressway.



The Shah Alam Expressway (KESAS) indirectly serves residents of Puchong Perdana, a residential area in Puchong, Selangor, Malaysia. Although KESAS doesn't directly cross Puchong Perdana, it offers accessibility through nearby interchanges like Puchong Utama and Bukit Jalil. Residents can use these entry and exit points to connect to KESAS, enabling travel to various destinations within the Klang Valley, such as Shah Alam, Subang Jaya, Klang, and Petaling Jaya. Despite not running through Puchong Perdana, KESAS plays a crucial role in improving overall accessibility for residents by integrating them into the broader transportation network of the Klang Valley.
